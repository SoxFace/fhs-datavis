Learning & Teaching Salutes Staff Success

Reflecting on Teaching in 2014, Semester 2

Using USE data, we identified units of study

Overall Success Criteria

    Overall:

  • Units have scored over 85-90% agreement (agree plus strongly agree) on all questions in the University’s Unit of Study Evaluation (USE) form and a 60% or higher response rate.
  • 50%-85% agreement
  • 0-50% agreement

    • Criteria:

  • Assessment: Units have scored over 85-90% agreement (agree plus strongly agree) on Q5 “The assessment in this unit of study allowed me to demonstrate what I had understood”, over 80-90% Q12 ‘overall satisfaction’ and have a 60% or higher response rate.
  • Feedback and communication: over 85-90% agreement (agree plus strongly agree) on questions Q4, Q7, Q8 and Q10, over 80-90% Q12 ‘overall satisfaction’ and have a 60% or higher response rate.
  • Engagement with the learning activities: over over 85-90% agreement (agree plus strongly agree) on Q4 and over 85-90% agreement (agree plus strongly agree) Q12 ‘overall satisfaction’ and have a 60% or higher response rate and over 40% response rate.
